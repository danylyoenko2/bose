FAQ

01 How to pair with a mobile device?

To pair with a mobile device, turn on your headphones, enable Bluetooth on your device, and select the Bose NC 700 from the available devices list.

02 What is the wireless range of the Bose NC 700?
The wireless range of the Bose NC 700 headphones is up to 10 meters (33 feet) in an open space.
03 How do I fold the Bose NC 700 headphones for storage?

The Bose NC 700 headphones do not fold completely but the earcups rotate to lay flat for easy storage in the provided case.

04 Does the guarantee work internationally?

Yes, the guarantee works internationally, but terms and conditions may vary depending on the region.
